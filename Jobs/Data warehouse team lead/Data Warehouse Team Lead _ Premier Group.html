Data Warehouse Team Lead

Central London

Do you have experience of leading a team? I have an excellent opportunity for a Data Warehouse Team Leader.  This is working for a huge global FinTech firm based in Central London. The business transforms the way financial institutions serve their wealth management customers is looking for a highly skilled Data Warehouse Team Lead to join their team.

You will be co-ordinating the activity of the team in regards to providing support, developing tasks and liaising with the project managers.

The ideal candidate will have:

  • Lead a team before
  • Both data warehousing and data warehousing design experience Database, ETL and Universe Design
  • Data modelling experience
  • Proven SQL experience

You must have previous experience of leading a team!
