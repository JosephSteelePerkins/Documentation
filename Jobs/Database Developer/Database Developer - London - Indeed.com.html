Database Developer

Understanding Recruitment
-
London
£39,000 - £43,500 a year -  Permanent
Apply Now
Apply On Company Site
A brand new role has just arisen for an experienced Database Developer to join a very important charity working remotely.. As the Database Developer, you will be taking various specifications from around the business where you will then create technical designs to meet these as you will have full control over the projects and be encouraged to develop innovative solutions.

The Database is the live and blood of this charity which is based on Blackbaud CRM system. As the Database Developer you will be part of major development projects to enhance the use of the system and to integrate different functions of the charity. This charity hugely believes in personal development & work life balance so you will have the chance to learn a lot of skills whilst utilising the most of the working from home and flexi hours.

Skills required for the Database Developer are:

  • Strong development knowledge in SQL
  • Ability to work with stakeholders to understand technical requirements
  • Experience working with SSIS and ETL packages
  • Experience working with the development life cycle

Developer / BI Developer / Database Developer / Data Analyst / SQL / CRM

Salary: £36,000 - £39,200 + Fantastic benefits

Location: Remote, but infrequent travel to Old Street, London, may be required

This role starts as a 6 month FTC but with the opportunity to go permanent after.

Apply now for further details and immediate consideration.
