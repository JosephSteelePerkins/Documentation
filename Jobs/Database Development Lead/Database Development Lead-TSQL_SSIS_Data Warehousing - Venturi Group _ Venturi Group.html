    Database Development Lead-TSQL/SSIS/Data Warehousing

    Business Intelligence & Data Warehousing | £55k - 65k per year + bens | England, Greater London, London

    Database Development Lead–TSQL/SSIS/Data Warehousing required by a Leading Household Company in its Central London offices.  This Boutique Company works with a vast number of globally recognised House-hold names and as a Database Development Lead you will work on a variety of innovative projects.  This Company is currently running SQL Server 2017 Enterprise Edition which it upgraded to on the first day of its release, and it is constantly investing in the latest technologies.

    As a Database Development Lead–TSQL/SSIS/Data Warehousing you will be responsible for designing, delivering and supporting customer driven databases, data warehouses and data integrations for blue chip customers.   You will also manage a team of 3 other database developers, design and implement solutions and develop testing solutions for those applications, so you ideally have management or mentoring experience. 

    Key Responsibilities of the Database Development Lead– TSQL/SSIS/Data Warehousing

    • Design and develop BI solutions in line with business requirements and the IT strategy, primarily using TSQL and SSIS
      • Leading and creating high quality, reusable SQL Server procedures, triggers, scripts, views, databases and related SQL-based components to support the effective delivery of software solutions.
      • Have input to wider architectural decisions as required
      • Lead the design and development of customer facing applications
      • Implement database design to support current and new applications
      • Design new test cases and conduct regression testing, advise on errors and recommend solutions
      • Manage/mentor other members of the team, providing guidance and sharing technical knowledge
      • Analyse and review designs and work requests to ensure best use of technology deployment
      • Provide a good level of project management to ensure the efficient and timely delivery of development projects for key clients
      • Take responsibility for the use and improvement of development practices

    Key Skills of the Database Development Lead–TSQL/SSIS/Data Warehousing

    • Experience of SQL Server 2008 or above (ideally SQL Server 2016 Enterprise Edition)
      • Extensive commercial experience of SQL development on Microsoft SQL Server, including the development of stored procedures, triggers, views, indexation, database schema design, query tuning, problem investigation / fault diagnosis, and controlled deployment and release of SQL code
      • Extensive experience developing ETL packages using SSIS
      • Experience as a mentor/team lead or Manager in a Database development role
      • Experience of producing solutions for both OLTP and Data Warehouse environments

    As a Database Development Lead you will receive a base salary of up to £65,000 plus benefits. This Company is doing some exciting things currently, so it is an excellent time to join!

     If the Database Development Lead role sounds like something you are interested in please email a recent version of your CV to Danielle.strachan@venturi-group.com.  Alternatively apply within.
